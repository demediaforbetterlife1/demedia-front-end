# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: March 6, 2026**

DeMEDIA ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Name, username, email, phone number, date of birth
- **Profile Information**: Bio, profile picture, cover photo, location, interests
- **Content**: Posts, stories, comments, messages, photos, videos
- **Payment Information**: Billing details for premium features (processed securely)

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, browser type, IP address
- **Usage Data**: Pages viewed, features used, time spent, interactions
- **Location Data**: Approximate location based on IP address (if permitted)
- **Cookies**: Session data, preferences, analytics

### 1.3 Information from Third Parties
- **Social Media**: If you connect social accounts, we receive public profile information
- **Analytics**: Aggregated data from analytics providers
- **Payment Processors**: Transaction confirmation (not full payment details)

## 2. How We Use Your Information

### 2.1 Service Provision
- Create and manage your account
- Display your profile and content to other users
- Enable communication between users
- Process transactions for premium features
- Provide customer support

### 2.2 Service Improvement
- Analyze usage patterns to improve features
- Develop new features and services
- Conduct research and analytics
- Test new features with user groups

### 2.3 Safety and Security
- **Content Moderation**: Scan content for prohibited material
- **Fraud Prevention**: Detect and prevent fraudulent activity
- **Security**: Protect against unauthorized access
- **Compliance**: Comply with legal obligations

### 2.4 Communication
- Send service-related notifications
- Respond to your inquiries
- Send marketing communications (with consent)
- Notify you of policy changes

## 3. Content Moderation and Safety

### 3.1 Automated Scanning
- All uploaded content is automatically scanned for:
  - Violence and graphic content
  - Sexual or explicit material
  - Nudity or revealing content
  - Hate speech and harassment
  - Illegal activities
  - Copyright violations

### 3.2 AI-Powered Detection
- Machine learning models analyze images and videos
- Text content is analyzed for prohibited language
- Behavioral patterns are monitored for suspicious activity
- Flagged content is queued for human review

### 3.3 Human Review
- Trained moderators review flagged content
- Reports from users are investigated
- Context is considered in moderation decisions
- Appeals can be submitted for removed content

### 3.4 Data Retention for Safety
- Removed content is retained for 90 days for appeals
- Violation records are kept for enforcement
- Serious violations are reported to authorities
- IP addresses of violators are logged

- We never sell personal information to third parties
- We do not share data with advertisers

The following is visible to other users:

- Public posts and stories
- Comments and interactions
- Follower/following lists (if public)

## 5. Your Privacy Rights

### 5.1 Access and Control
- **View Your Data**: Request a copy of your data
- **Update Information**: Edit your profile and settings
- **Download Data**: Export your content and information
- **Delete Account**: Permanently remove your account

### 5.2 Privacy Settings

- **Story Privacy**: Control who sees your stories
- **Post Privacy**: Set visibility for each post
- **Message Settings**: Control who can message you

### 5.3 Communication Preferences
- **Email Notifications**: Opt out of marketing emails
- **Push Notifications**: Control app notifications
- **SMS**: Opt out of text messages
- **In-App Notifications**: Customize notification types

### 5.4 Data Rights (GDPR/CCPA)
- **Right to Access**: Request your data

- **Right to Portability**: Receive data in portable format

## 6. Data Security

### 6.1 Security Measures
- **Encryption**: Data encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest
- **Access Controls**: Strict employee access limitations
- **Authentication**: Secure login with optional 2FA
- **Monitoring**: 24/7 security monitoring and alerts
- **Audits**: Regular security audits and penetration testing

### 6.2 Your Responsibility
- Use strong, unique passwords
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Keep your device secure
- Log out on shared devices
- Report suspicious activity immediately

### 6.3 Data Breaches
- We will notify affected users within 72 hours
- Authorities will be notified as required by law
- We will provide guidance on protective measures
- Incident reports will be published transparently

## 7. Data Retention

### 7.1 Active Accounts
- Account data: Retained while account is active
- Content: Retained until you delete it
- Messages: Retained for 1 year or until deleted
- Analytics: Aggregated data retained indefinitely

### 7.2 Deleted Accounts
- Account data: Deleted within 30 days
- Content: Removed from public view immediately
- Backups: Purged within 90 days
- Legal holds: Retained as required by law

### 7.3 Inactive Accounts
- Accounts inactive for 2+ years may be deleted
- Warning email sent 30 days before deletion
- Data can be recovered within 30-day grace period
